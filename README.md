# Supply_Chain_Management_Dashboard_PowerBI
## Project Overview:
A supply chain analytics dashboard covering sourcing, manufacturing, Sales, and logistic insights,
This project visulizes the supply chain process for makeup products, and help me to improve my analyzing and visulaization skills
## Objective:
The Goal of this project: Analyze and Understand products performance, sales, manufacturing, tranportation and analyze total costs, total revenue, defect rates, stock levels, products quantity, and lead time etc.
## Problem Statment:
  - Analyze Which Carrier is genrating the lowest revenue?
  - Which product category has the highest defect rates?
  - Which transportation mode shows a highest defect rate?
  - Which product category is generating the lowest sales quantity and needs attention?
  - Which transportation modes are the most cost-effective?
  - Which shipping carrier has the highest shipping cost?
  - Which SKUs are generating the lowest revenue and need improvment?
  - Which SKUs have the lowest stock levels and are at risk of stock-out?
  - Which SKUs have the highest order quantity(High-demand products)?
  - Which product category has the highest manufacturing cost?
  - Which product category has the longest lead time cousing delivery delays?

## Key Findings and Insights:
**Lowest revenue generated carriers:** Carriers A

**Top defect rates products:** Skincare products

**Top defect rates by Tranportation:** Road and Rail

**less quantity saling products:** Cosmetics 

**Top tranportation modes by costs:** Road and rail

**Highest shipping cost's shipping carrier:** Carrier B

**Lowest revenue generated SKU:** SKU 59, SKU 86, SKU 78

**Lowest stock level SKU:** SKU 33, SKU 24, SKU 87

**Highest order quantity SKU:** SKU 0, SKU 90, SKU 33, SKU 19

**Highest manufacturing cost Product category:** Skincare products

**Longest lead time Product category:** Haircare

## Recommendation:
1- Focus on low-revenue carrier(Carrier A)
Carrier A's low revenue contribution indicates poor performance. The company should review their service quality, reduce dependancy on them, and consider cost negotiations or switching to more reliable carriers like Carrier B.

2- Reduce manufacturing cost for Skincare products
Implement cost optimization strategies such as supplier negotiation and production effciency improvment. 

3- Improve delivery efficiency for Haircare products
Haircare has the highest lead time, optimize logistic planning, reduce bottlenecks in supply chain, and increase stock levels in advance. 

4- Investigate high defect rates in Skincare products
Conduct root-couse analysis(QA checks, packaging quality) to reduse product returns and improve customer satisfaction.

5- Promote low selling Cosmetics category
Launch targeted promotional campaigns or give offers and Discounts to boost sales volume.  

6- Optimize shipping costs by evaluating carriers
Carrier B has higher shipping costs but it also generated high revenue, we have to give shipments to Carrier B and ask Carrier B to reduce cost per delivery, Mayby they would be agree because they will get more business. 

7- Prevent stock-outs for low-stock SKUs
Improve inventory forecasting and restocking for SKUs with low stock levels to prevent lost sales.

8- Utilize high-demand SKUs for marketing
Highlight top-ordered products in ads and cross-sell them to maximize revenue.

9- Enhance Road and Rail transportation Quality
Since defect issues are high in shipping, implement better handling practics and packaging standards during transit. 

## Conclusion
Carrier B and Skincare products are performing really well, but cosmetics and carrier A need improvment. Also, road and rail transport seem to cause more defects. by improving delivery handling and product quality, the business can increase revenue and customer satisfaction.
